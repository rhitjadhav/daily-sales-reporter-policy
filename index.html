Privacy Policy for Daily Sales Reporter

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Sales Reporter ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect include:

  • Information You Provide to Us:
    • Account Information: When you register as a pump owner, we collect your name, your petrol pump's name, and your phone number for authentication and account setup.
    • User-Generated Content: We collect the data you input into the app, including sales reports (opening/closing readings, rates, sales amounts), cash and digital payment details, credit sales (customer name, amount), expenses, nozzle configurations, stock entries (fuel type, quantity, price), and information about your DSMs (name, phone number).
  • Information We Collect Automatically:
    • Usage Information: We use Google services (like Firebase Analytics) to collect information about how you interact with our App, such as features used and time spent in the app.
    • Device Information: We collect device-specific information, such as your device model and operating system version. Firebase Cloud Messaging collects a registration token to send you push notifications.
    • Advertising ID: We use Google AdMob to serve ads, which may collect your device's advertising ID to provide personalized advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our App;
  • Process transactions, including in-app subscriptions and purchases;
  • Send you push notifications for important events, like new report submissions;
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our App;
  • Understand and analyze how you use our App;
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the App, and for marketing and promotional purposes;
  • Serve advertisements through Google AdMob.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors and service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as Firebase (for database, authentication, and hosting) and Google AdMob (for advertising).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

4. Data Security

We use Firebase's security features to help protect your information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. We aim to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data.

  • Access, Update, or Delete: You can manage your account information and some data (like DSMs and reports) directly within the app. For other requests, please contact us.
  • Consent: You can manage your consent for personalized advertising through the consent message presented in the app or through your device's settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and within our app.

8. Contact Us
